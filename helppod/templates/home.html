About Us

HelpPod is a personal concierge application which will complete any requested task through effective matching of clients with HelpPod helpers. It helps to maintain work-life balance of our clients’ busy schedules and add hours to their days. Meanwhile, it also occupies the abeyant population by giving them with the task opportunities that would otherwise go missed. Through our platform, busy people will be able to share their workload with people who have plenty time to spare – and thus, manage to form a bridge between the two. HelpPod will help our clients get a personal assistant on demand to do anything and everything at any time. It’s a one-stop shop for all your task needs where we solve your daily and iterating chores while you focus on things that lie close to your heart.
